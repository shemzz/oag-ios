One Africa Global Foundation is a development arm of One Africa Global and a non-government, not-for-profit and nonsectarian, non-partisan organization working for social development of vulnerable and less privileged people. We work towards Building the Continent through innovation, Sound Health, Empowerment and Education.

VISION

OAGF envisions a "poverty-free, empowered & democratic society in Africa; which is guided by the values of equity, accountability, inclusion and respect”

MISSION

One Africa Global Foundation's Mission is to work for the development & empowerment of socio-economically disadvantaged & vulnerable people in Africa and assist them in addressing their poverty-linked issues, lack of access to quality education and health complications.

LEGAL STATUS

One Africa Global Foundation is a registered body. The initiative is registered under “The NGO/CSO's Registration Act, 1999 of the Federal republic of Nigeria, In the US and UK. Its proudly endorsed by African Union ECOSOCC.

EDUCATE ONEAFRICA

EducateOneAfrica is a Scholarship service provider changing the narratives of African Education System. University is expensive, from tuition to accommodation and welfare. We know and understand this challenges. In fact, most young people struggle till graduation while a hand full drop out as a result of inability to pay their fees. This is why One Africa Global created EducateOneAfrica. If you want scholarships as an African then you need EducateOneAfrica There is free money for university out there, and EducateOneAfrica will help students just like you win funding for scholarship. At EducateOneAfrica, we provide access to university funding for Africans in need. EducateOneAfrica is in partnership with leading University globally.

One Africa Global Foundation has remained engaged in education sector since year 2015. The foundation has joined in effort in improving education system in Africa. Her latest flagship project in education is the “EdcuateOneAfrica). The EducateOneAfrica Project is one that is aimed at helping scholarship find Africans.

TREAT ONEAFRICA

TreatOneAfrica is a healthcare service provider changing the narratives of African healthcare delivery. At TreatOneAfrica , we provide access to medical treatment to Africans in need and in critical conditions. TreatOneAfrica is in partnership with leading hospitals and experienced physicians that are reputable, professionals and licensed. The significance of this initiative is to organically reach out to Africans with critical health through the provision of reputable hospitals and funding of medical bills.

Health is an essential aspect of life and as such, it defines the total wellbeing of an individual. From One Africa Global Foundation we have developed a project called TreatOneAfrica. Treat One Africa is a free health care services provider design for African to get well at zero cost.
