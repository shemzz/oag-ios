1Music1Sound is another component of One Africa Global. It is a smaller event than that of OAMF, usually an intimate setting with an audience of 1,500-3,500 people. The beauty of 1Music1Sound is the bridging of the gap from different music genres, styles and/or cultures depending on the line up.

GOAL

The goal is to share one night with music lovers who may not necessarily know everyone on the bill but will equally have an enjoyable time and hopefully be exposed to a new style of music or artist. Each line up is hand selected to ensure a quality show with the most talented and experienced artists from across the world. The most recent 1M1S was held in Dubai at Jumeirah Creekside Hotel was very successful. We showcased beautiful musical diversity, our lineup was Afrobeats biggest sensation Dbanj, Jamaica's hottest act Kranium, Living legend and R&B King Ginuwine, Praiz and Styl-Plus. The audience was able to enjoy longer performances because the lineup is not like OAMF which has over 20 acts each time. 1Music1Sound shows that music doesn't need to be translated to be enjoyed, it is a universal sound meant to be loved by all.
